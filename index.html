base-lol - encode binary data as emojis

Encodes binary data as a sequence of emojis in UTF-8. Ready for embedding in tweets, file-names, WiFi SSIDs etc!

As an example, the byte sequence 0xcafecafe becomes 💋💿💋💿. This SVG (gzipped) becomes:
